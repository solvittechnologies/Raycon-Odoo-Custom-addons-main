100% legal

Odoo Sign is as legally binding as "wet" signature

By law, eSignatures are similar to a handwritten signature on a paper document. All documents signed with Odoo Sign are supported by several pieces of legislation : 2000 U.S. Electronic Signature (ESIGN), Uniform Electronic Transactions Act (UETA) and European Directive (EC/1999/93).
